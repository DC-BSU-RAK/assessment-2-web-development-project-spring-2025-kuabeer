Genshin Impact 5.6 Update!

New Character

Escoffier (Cryo): 5-Star Character "Tasteful Excellence"

The ex-head chef of Hotel Debord, renowned throughout Fontaine as the Patissiere Supreme and vanguard of precision gastronomy. Has incomparably strict standards when it comes to cooking.

Ifa (Anemo): 4-Star Character "In the Wake of Wandering Winds"

A vet from the Flower-Feather Clan who cherishes the life he leads alongside all manner of living things — people, animals, you name it.

New Main Story

New Archon Quest: Chapter V Act VI – A Thousand Fathoms Beneath

The new Archon quest continues the Fontaine saga with Act VI, titled A Thousand Fathoms Beneath. Travelers will uncover the secrets of the Primordial Sea and face off against a new boss tied to the Abyss Order.

New Event: Whirling Waltz

Whirling Waltz is a vibrant limited-time event set in Fontaine, featuring a mix of mini-games, story-driven quests, and light puzzles. Players can participate to earn Primogems, materials, and unlock the exclusive 4-star bow, Sequence of Solitude.
