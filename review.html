Wiztako ★★★★☆

Overall it is good enough, the artstyle, animations, combat, and story are nice, but there's one thing that decreases the score: Story pacing and Gacha.Even though I like the story, it is simply too slow, you have to stand there, watching the characters speak for literal hours just to complete a single quest, I feel it is more "standing there clicking to skip dialogue" than doing the quest itself. denni5m ★★★☆☆

Free or not, that means nothing to me I don't rate games based on how much they cost anyway. This is an annoying game because it could have been great but the story just does nothing to help to pull you along and the combat is very shallow. Also the basic game loop of exploring and leveling is ok but gets repetitive very quickly. Is a shame because if they just added some depth to the combat and made the missions scale to you level instead of insisting on massive leaps in level between missions it would be a good game.
